About Me

Hello! I'm a sophomore at IIT Bombay, pursuing a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering. My journey at IITB has been a rollercoaster of learning, friendships, and unforgettable moments. From late-night coding sessions to vibrant fests, here's a glimpse of my life on campus!

Milestones

July 2024: First Day at IITB

Stepped into the lush green campus, nervous yet excited. Met my hostel mates and explored the iconic Main Building.

December 2024: Mood Indigo Fest

Volunteered for Mood Indigo, IITB's cultural fest. Danced, laughed, and made lifelong friends.

Summer 2025: First Internship

Interned at a tech startup, applying my coding skills to real-world problems. A huge learning curve!

Learnings & Growth

At IITB, I've grown both academically and personally. I've completed 62 credits, joined the Coding Club, and working on various project. My favorite course so far is Data Structures, which sharpened my problem-solving skills.
